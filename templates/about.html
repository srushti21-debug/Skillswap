About SkillSwap 🌍

Where learning meets sharing — grow your skills while helping others grow theirs.

Our Mission 🚀

SkillSwap was built with a simple idea: learning should be free, collaborative, and fun. We connect people who want to learn new skills with those who can teach — no money, just mutual growth.

🤝 Community First

Our users form a supportive community that believes in learning through connection and exchange.

🎯 Verified Skills

We ensure trust through verified skills, certificates, and active engagement badges.

💡 Continuous Growth

With progress tracking and challenges, every swap helps you learn something new.

Frequently Asked Questions ❓

How do I start swapping skills?

Create a profile, list what you can teach and what you want to learn, then browse others to find your match!

Is SkillSwap free to use?

Yes! SkillSwap is completely free — we promote mutual learning without money barriers.

How do I verify my skills?

Upload certificates or get peer verification from other learners on the platform.
